RussNeft Group adheres to the principle of responsible attitude of business to the society. The Group aims to promote development of the economy of the regions and growth of their residents’ well-being in every possible way. The holding assigns substantial tax payments to local budgets, creates new jobs, implements large-scale social programs on the basis of agreements with administrations of constituent entities of the Federation.
