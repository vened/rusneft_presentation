Geology and Reserves

Key 2013 results in Geology:

  • New oil deposit was discovered at Zapadno-Varioganskoye field. Oil deposit borders at Roslavlskoye, Verkhne-Shapshinskoye and Tagrinskoye fields were extended.
  • Mapping of Zapadno-Varioganskoye and Duklinskoye fields with 3D seismic was completed in 2013.
  • Exploration wells were drilled at Zapadno-Varioganskoye and Untygeyskoye fields in KhMAO as well as at Dintemskoye license in Udmurtia.
