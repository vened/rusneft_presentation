RussNeft Group of Companies is a vertically - integrated oil holding.

The structure of RussNeft Group includes 45 upstream companies.

Subsidiaries of the Group are located in 16 regions of Russia, CIS countries and West Africa.

The head office of the company is in Moscow.

The Company’s personnel is 21 000 employees.

RussNeft performs exploration works and produces oil and condensate at 208 licenses.

The total amount of oil production output by the companies of RussNeft Group is 17.0 million tons.

Oil net effective pay of RussNeft Group exceeds 765 million tons.

8th

among the oil and condensate producers in Russia
